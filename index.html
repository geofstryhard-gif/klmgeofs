About our virtual airline

We simulate KLM operations in GeoFS: real-world-inspired routes, fleet discipline, and events that balance realism and fun. Our focus: safety, airmanship, and community.

  • Ops style: SID/STAR when practical, hand-flown visuals encouraged
  • ATC: Structured phraseology during events, flexible in casual flights
  • PIREP: Simple flight reports with screenshots/logs
  • Community: Discord-based briefings, mentoring, and crew rooms
